Our commitment
Google’s mission is to organise the world’s information and make it universally accessible and useful. This is more relevant than ever in today’s mobile-first world. In India, we want to bring the Internet to life for everyone. The more people have access to the tools, services, information and training online, the more they can pursue their ambitions and reach their potential.

We want to help Indians connect to the Internet that serves their needs and helps them make a positive impact on their communities. We aim to achieve this with programs and products with Indian user at its core.
